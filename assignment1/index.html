/01. INITIAL FOCUS

[Q. 01] What was the first thing you paid attention to when interacting with the experience?

The original mouse icon has been replaced with a small circle that includes a motion trail, enhancing the cursor's visibility for users due to its compact size.

On the homepage, the designer introduces himself through a high-quality video, paired with the bold, uppercase title "Rich Brown," which prominently stands out.

The overall design is clean, modern, and minimalist, which reflects an intuitive, straightforward, and professional approach that effectively showcases the designer's personal style.

/02. 2-MINUTE INTERACTION

[Q. 02] Spend two minutes with the experience and create a list of each of your discrete actions.

  • Move the mouse around the homepage to interact with the motion trail.
  • Select the contact information button.
  • Scroll down to explore the page content.
  • Notice text and images tilting left-right based on scroll direction, but it's unclear what the information is.
  • Select and skip through the introduction video, then close it.
  • Hover over the Bathmate image, moving the mouse around its border to see the 3D tilting motion.
  • In the project overview, the background color changes with each project image hovered over.
  • Hover through the project archive, mainly viewing images to understand project content, then scroll back for details.
  • Click on the Bathmate image to navigate to a new page with detailed project information.
  • Scroll through the Bathmate project detail page, which moves horizontally, skipping text.
  • Mistakenly thought the "--|" was a navigation arrow.
  • Look for a button to return to the homepage, thinking the rotating icon in the top right might be it, clicking surrounding icons to test, then find that the "R" button on the top right is -the way back to the homepage.
  • Click the "B" button, see too much text, and close it.
